The Heading Elements

The Heading elements are titles or subtitles for the HTML document. It displays a blank line before its contents and a blank line after its contents.

There are 6 Heading tags; h1 thru h6. The h1 tag is the main and largest heading and the others are for smaller headings. The h6 tag is the smallest heading.
